Who We Are

Simplifying UAE Government Processes

Afaq Al-Hind is a trusted and experienced document clearing and PRO (Public Relations Officer) services company based in the UAE. We specialize in handling all types of government documentation, immigration procedures, business licensing, and legal document services for individuals, families, and businesses.

Our team brings deep knowledge of UAE government systems and procedures, allowing us to navigate the process efficiently on your behalf. Whether you're applying for a Golden Visa, setting up a business, getting certificates attested, or renewing your Emirates ID, we handle it all with speed and professionalism.

We believe in transparency, reliability, and personal service. Every client receives dedicated attention, clear communication, and honest advice. Our goal is to make the UAE documentation process as smooth and stress-free as possible for you.
